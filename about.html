I am a first generation American who grew up in Connecticut. Then lived a few years in NYC until the pandemic also rerouted all my life plans.

Trying to make the best of a terrible time in my life I decided to buy a trailer, convert it, and then travel the country in it. While this might have been a crazy thought at the time it was far from being out of character for me. From moving to NYC on my own, to starting hormones at the age of 18 and identifying as a trans woman, to then road tripping across country solo to San Diego, I have always travelled off the beaten path.

This has lead me to another adventure; learning to code and be a front-end developer. Currently dedicating all my time to learning and fully grasping this new language.

This is only the beginning and I hope to lift my trans community by bringing visibilty to their work via my front-end development.
